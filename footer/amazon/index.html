About us

Along with being Earth’s Best Employer, we’re also committed to becoming Earth’s Safest Place to Work. Across the country, Amazon’s team of around 75,000 people carry out hundreds of different roles at sites and offices, large and small—and nothing is more important than their safety in the workplace. We have been turning this commitment into reality through global investments of more than $1 billion in safety initiatives, technologies and programmes since 2019.
